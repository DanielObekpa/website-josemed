Why Regular Checkups Matter

Routine medical checkups help detect potential health issues before they become serious. Early detection gives you the best chance for getting the right treatment quickly and avoiding complications.

What’s Included in Our General Checkup:

  • ✅ Full Body Examination
  • ✅ Blood Pressure Check
  • ✅ Blood Sugar Level Test
  • ✅ Cholesterol Test
  • ✅ BMI & Weight Assessment
  • ✅ Doctor Consultation
  • ✅ Free Follow-Up Call

Who Needs a Regular Checkup?

  • ✔️ Adults aged 40 and above
  • ✔️ People with diabetes or hypertension
  • ✔️ Office workers with sedentary lifestyle
  • ✔️ Anyone experiencing stress or fatigue
